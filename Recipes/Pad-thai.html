Pad Thai Recipe

Back to Home

This Pad Thai recipe brings the authentic flavors of Thailand to your kitchen. Easy to make and bursting with flavor!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup sliced shallots
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu
  • 1/4 cup tamarind paste
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, crushed
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Directions

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the shallots and garlic, and stir-fry until fragrant.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side, crack the eggs into the pan, and scramble until cooked through.
  4. Add the chicken, shrimp, or tofu to the pan, followed by the cooked rice noodles.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the tamarind paste, fish sauce, brown sugar, and rice vinegar. Pour the sauce over the noodles and toss to coat.
  6. If desired, sprinkle with chili flakes for extra heat.
  7. Serve the Pad Thai with crushed roasted peanuts, fresh cilantro, and lime wedges.
