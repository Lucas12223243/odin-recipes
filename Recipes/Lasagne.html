This easy lasagna recipe is perfect for a delicious homemade meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 (14oz) cans chopped tomatoes
  • 2 (6oz) cans tomato paste
  • 1 tsp salt

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta or cottage cheese
  • Â¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 6 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet, cook ground beef until browned, drain fat.
  3. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, seasonings, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 35-45 minutes. Remove foil and bake 10 more minutes.
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with parsley.
